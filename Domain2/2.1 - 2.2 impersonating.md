# 🔐 Security Study Guide: Impersonation & Identity Fraud

---

## 1️⃣ What is Impersonation?

### 🧾 Definition:

**Impersonation** is when an attacker **pretends to be someone else** to gain trust and obtain information 

---

### 🎯 Goal:

* Make you trust them
* Get you to give:

  * Passwords
  * Financial info
  * Personal data

---

### ⚠️ Key Idea:

👉 Trust is the attack vector

---

---

# 2️⃣ How Impersonation Works

---

## 🎭 The Attack Structure

Every impersonation attack has:

1. **Actor** (attacker)
2. **Pretext (story)**

👉 The attacker tells a believable story to trick you

---

## 🧠 Example Pretexts:

* “I’m from Microsoft support”
* “This is the US Treasury”
* “You qualify for a credit offer”

---

### ⚠️ Key Pattern:

👉 Authority + urgency = compliance

---

---

# 3️⃣ Common Impersonation Techniques

---

## 🧑‍💻 Pretending to Be IT Support

* “We need your password to fix your system”

👉 ⚠️ Real IT will NEVER ask for your password

---

---

## 🧑‍💼 Pretending to Be Management

* “I’m the VP—send me this info”

👉 Uses **authority pressure**

---

---

## 🧠 Using Technical Language

* Confuses victim
* Sounds legitimate

---

---

# 4️⃣ Information Elicitation

---

## 🧾 Definition:

**Eliciting information** = tricking someone into giving sensitive details

---

### 🎯 Goal:

* Credit card number
* Bank info
* Social Security Number (SSN)

---

### ⚠️ Key Idea:

👉 They don’t ask directly
👉 They build a story first

---

---

# 5️⃣ Vishing (Voice Phishing)

---

## 🧾 Definition:

**Vishing = Voice Phishing**

---

### 🎯 How it works:

* Phone call
* Fake identity
* Requests sensitive info

---

### 📌 Example:

* “Your payment failed, give us your card details”

---

---

# 6️⃣ Identity Fraud

---

## 🧾 Definition:

Using someone else’s personal information to commit fraud

---

---

## 💳 Credit Card Fraud

* Attacker opens account in your name
* Uses their address
* Spends money

---

---

## 🏦 Bank Fraud

* Opens bank account under your name
* Uses it for illegal transactions

---

---

## 🧾 Tax Fraud

* Files taxes as you
* Steals refund

---

---

### ⚠️ Key Idea:

👉 You take the damage
👉 Attacker takes the money

---

---

# 7️⃣ Why Impersonation Works

---

👉 Because people:

* Trust authority
* Don’t verify identity
* Feel pressured to act fast

---

---

# 8️⃣ How to Prevent Impersonation

---

## 🚫 Do NOT:

* Give passwords
* Share personal info
* Trust unsolicited calls

---

---

## ✅ DO:

* Verify identity independently
* Call official number
* Follow company procedures

---

---

### 🔐 Verification Process

👉 Always confirm:

* Who is calling
* Where they are from
