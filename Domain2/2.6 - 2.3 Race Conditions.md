# 🔐 2.3: Race Conditions & TOCTOU Attacks

---

## 1️⃣ What is a Race Condition?

---

### 🧾 Definition:

A **race condition** occurs when two or more operations happen at nearly the same time, and the application does not properly handle their order or interaction

---

### 🎯 Core Idea:

👉 The system assumes things happen in order
👉 But in reality, they happen **simultaneously**

---

---

# 🧠 🏁 ANALOGY (THIS IS THE ONE YOU REMEMBER)

---

## 🎟️ Online Ticket Booking Analogy

There is **1 ticket left** for a concert

---

### 🧠 What happens:

1. User 1 clicks “Buy”
   👉 System checks → “1 ticket available” ✅

2. User 2 clicks at almost the same time
   👉 System ALSO checks → “1 ticket available” ✅

---

👉 ⚠️ The system has NOT updated yet

---

3. Both users complete purchase

---

## 💥 Result:

👉 Both receive confirmation

👉 BUT:

* Only **1 real ticket exists**
* One user will NOT be able to enter

---

### 🔥 What this means:

👉 The system allowed **two actions using the same resource**

---

---

# 2️⃣ Why It’s Called “Race”

---

👉 Multiple actions are **“racing”** to execute first

* Whoever finishes first affects outcome
* System may behave incorrectly

---

---

# 3️⃣ Why Race Conditions Are Dangerous

---

👉 They can cause:

* Incorrect data
* Duplicate actions
* Privilege escalation

---

### ⚠️ Key Idea:

👉 System state ≠ real state

---

---

# 4️⃣ TOCTOU Attack (VERY IMPORTANT)

---

## 🧾 Definition:

**TOCTOU = Time-Of-Check to Time-Of-Use**

---

### 🎯 Meaning:

👉 System:

1. Checks something
2. Uses it later

---

### ⚠️ Problem:

👉 Data may change in between

---

---

# 5️⃣ TOCTOU Analogy (Same Ticket Example)

---

## 🧠 Scenario:

1. System checks → “Ticket available” ✅
2. Delay happens
3. Another user buys the ticket
4. System still allows purchase

---

## 💥 Result:

👉 System used **outdated information**

---

---

# 6️⃣ Real Example (Bank Scenario)

---

* Account = $100

---

👉 Two withdrawals happen at same time:

* Both see $100
* Both withdraw

---

## ⚠️ Result:

👉 Incorrect balance (negative or wrong)

---

---

# 7️⃣ Key Terms (NO CONFUSION)

---

## 🧾 Race Condition

Simultaneous operations cause incorrect result

---

## 🧾 TOCTOU

Time-Of-Check to Time-Of-Use

---

## 🧾 Privilege Escalation

Gaining higher permissions

---

---

# 🧠 Memory Anchors (Concept-Based)

* Race = same time
* Ticket = resource
* Duplicate confirmation = system failure
* TOCTOU = check → delay → wrong result
