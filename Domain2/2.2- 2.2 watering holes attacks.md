
# 🔐 2.2: Watering Hole Attacks & Defense in Depth

---

## 1️⃣ What is a Watering Hole Attack?

### 🧾 Definition:

A **watering hole attack** is when an attacker compromises a **trusted website** that a target group frequently visits, and waits for victims to access it

---

### 🎯 Core Idea:

👉 Instead of attacking YOU directly
👉 The attacker attacks **a place you go to**

---

### ⚠️ Key Concept:

* Attacker “poisons” the site
* Victims visit normally
* Infection happens automatically

---

---

# 2️⃣ How a Watering Hole Attack Works

---

## 🧠 Step-by-Step

1. Attacker researches:

   * Websites target users visit

2. Attacker compromises that site

   * Finds vulnerability

3. Injects malicious code

   * Often JavaScript

4. Victim visits site

   * Gets infected

---

---

# 3️⃣ Why This Attack is Powerful

---

👉 It bypasses:

* Email filters
* User training
* Suspicious links

---

### ⚠️ Key Idea:

👉 User is doing **normal behavior**
👉 That’s why it’s dangerous

---

---

# 4️⃣ Targeted vs Broad Attacks

---

## 🎯 Targeted Attack

* Only specific users affected
* Based on:

  * IP address
  * Organization

---

## 🌍 Broad Attack

* Anyone visiting site is infected

---

---

# 5️⃣ Real-World Example (From Video)

---

* Financial institutions attacked
* Malicious JavaScript added
* Only certain organizations targeted

---

### ⚠️ Key Idea:

👉 Attackers can be VERY selective

---

---

# 6️⃣ What Makes This Possible?

---

## 🐛 Vulnerabilities

* Weak website security
* Outdated systems
* Poor configuration

---

---

# 7️⃣ Defense in Depth (VERY IMPORTANT)

---

## 🧾 Definition:

**Defense in depth** is a strategy that uses **multiple layers of security controls** to protect systems

---

---

## 🎯 Purpose:

👉 If one layer fails → another stops the attack

---

---

## 🛡️ Security Layers

---

### 🔥 Firewall

* Controls incoming/outgoing traffic

---

### 🧠 IPS (Intrusion Prevention System)

* IPS = **Intrusion Prevention System**
* Detects and blocks malicious traffic

---

### 🦠 Antivirus

* Detects malware on system

---

---

### ⚠️ Key Idea:

👉 No single control is enough

---

---

# 8️⃣ How Defense in Depth Works

---

## 🧠 Example Flow:

1. Firewall allows traffic
2. IPS analyzes traffic
3. Antivirus detects malware

👉 Attack stopped at different layer

---

---
