# 🔐 2.2: Phishing & Social Engineering

---

## 1️⃣ What is Phishing?

### 🧾 Definition:

**Phishing** is a type of **social engineering attack** that uses communication methods (email, text, phone) to trick users into believing something is legitimate when it is not 

---

### 🎯 Goal:

* Steal:

  * Username & password
  * Personal information
  * Financial data

---

### ⚠️ Key Idea:

👉 The attacker **pretends to be someone else** to gain trust

---

---

# 2️⃣ How Phishing Works

---

## 📧 Step-by-Step Attack

1. User receives message (email, SMS, call)

2. Message creates:

   * Urgency
   * Fear
   * Trust

3. User:

   * Clicks link OR
   * Enters credentials

4. Attacker:

   * Steals data OR
   * Installs malware

---

---

# 3️⃣ Common Phishing Techniques

---

## 🔗 Fake Links

* Looks legitimate
* Actually goes to malicious site

---

## 🧾 Fake Login Pages

* Looks like real site
* Slight differences:

  * Fonts
  * Spacing
  * Logos

---

## ⚠️ Red Flags

* Urgent message (“act now”)
* Threats (“account will be locked”)
* Suspicious sender

---

---

# 4️⃣ Email Spoofing

---

## 🧾 Definition:

**Spoofing** = pretending to be someone else

---

### 🎯 How it works:

* Fake email address
* Looks similar to real one

---

### 📌 Example:

* Real: `company.com`
* Fake: `cornpany.com`

---

---

# 5️⃣ Typosquatting

---

## 🧾 Definition:

Registering a domain name that is **slightly misspelled** to trick users

---

### 🎯 Example:

* Real: `professormesser.com`
* Fake: `professormessor.com`

---

### ⚠️ Key Idea:

👉 Small spelling difference = big risk

---

---

# 6️⃣ Pretexting

---

## 🧾 Definition:

Creating a **false story (pretext)** to trick someone into giving information

---

### 🎯 Example:

* “We are from your bank…”
* “Your payment failed…”

---

### ⚠️ Key Idea:

👉 Attacker builds a believable story

---

---

# 7️⃣ Types of Phishing

---

## 📞 Vishing

### 🧾 Definition:

**Vishing = Voice Phishing**

* Phone calls
* Caller ID spoofing

---

---

## 📱 Smishing

### 🧾 Definition:

**Smishing = SMS Phishing**
(SMS = Short Message Service)

---

* Text messages
* Malicious links

---

---

## 📧 Email Phishing

* Most common
* Uses links or attachments

---

---

# 8️⃣ What Happens After Phishing?

---

## 🔓 Account Takeover

* Attacker gets login access

---

## 💳 Financial Fraud

* Reset passwords
* Access bank accounts

---

## 🦠 Malware Infection

* Download malicious software

---

---

# 9️⃣ Why Phishing Works

---

👉 Because people:

* Trust messages
* Act quickly under pressure
* Don’t verify links

---

---

# 🔟 Common Phishing Scams

---

* Fake package delivery (USPS)
* Fake bank alerts
* Fake verification codes
* Fake payments

---
