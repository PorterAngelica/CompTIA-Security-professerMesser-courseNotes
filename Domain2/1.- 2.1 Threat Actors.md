# 🔐 2.1: Threat Actors

---

## 1️⃣ What is a Threat Actor?

### 🧾 Definition:

A **threat actor** is an entity responsible for actions that negatively impact security 

👉 Also called:

* Malicious actor

---

### 🎯 Purpose of Understanding Threat Actors:

* Identify **who is attacking**
* Understand **why (motivation)**
* Predict **how they attack**

---

## 2️⃣ Key Characteristics of Threat Actors

👉 When analyzing attackers, look at:

---

### 💰 Resources

* Low → limited tools
* High → advanced attacks

---

### 🧠 Sophistication

* Low → script users
* High → build their own tools

---

### 🎯 Motivation

* Data theft
* Disruption
* Espionage
* Political reasons
* Financial gain

---

### 🌍 Location

* Internal (inside organization)
* External (outside attackers)

---

## 🔥 KEY EXAM IDEA

👉 Questions will often ask:

* WHO is the attacker?
* WHAT is their motivation?
* HOW advanced are they?

---

# 3️⃣ Types of Threat Actors

---

## 🌍 Nation-State

### 🧾 Description:

* Government-backed attackers

---

### 💰 Resources:

* VERY HIGH

---

### 🧠 Sophistication:

* VERY HIGH

---

### 🎯 Motivation:

* Espionage
* Political goals
* War / disruption

---

### ⚠️ Key Term:

* **APT (Advanced Persistent Threat)**

---

## 👶 Unskilled Attacker (Script Kiddie)

### 🧾 Description:

* Uses tools/scripts without understanding

---

### 💰 Resources:

* LOW

---

### 🧠 Sophistication:

* LOW

---

### 🎯 Motivation:

* Curiosity
* Disruption
* Learning

---

## 🎭 Hacktivist

### 🧾 Description:

* Hacker with political or social agenda

---

### 💰 Resources:

* LOW–MEDIUM

---

### 🧠 Sophistication:

* MEDIUM–HIGH

---

### 🎯 Motivation:

* Political
* Ideological

---

### 📌 Common Attacks:

* Website defacement
* Data leaks
* DoS attacks

---

## 🧑‍💼 Insider Threat

### 🧾 Description:

* Internal employee or user

---

### 💰 Resources:

* MEDIUM (has internal access)

---

### 🧠 Sophistication:

* MEDIUM

---

### 🎯 Motivation:

* Revenge
* Financial gain

---

### ⚠️ Key Idea:

👉 Already has access → VERY dangerous

---

## 💵 Organized Crime

### 🧾 Description:

* Criminal groups working for profit

---

### 💰 Resources:

* HIGH

---

### 🧠 Sophistication:

* HIGH

---

### 🎯 Motivation:

* MONEY

---

### 📌 Common Attacks:

* Ransomware
* Data theft
* Fraud

---

## 🕶️ Shadow IT

### 🧾 Description:

* Employees bypassing IT policies

---

### 💰 Resources:

* LOW–MEDIUM

---

### 🧠 Sophistication:

* LOW–MEDIUM

---

### 🎯 Motivation:

* Convenience
* Avoid restrictions

---

### ⚠️ Key Risk:

👉 No security controls → big vulnerability

---

# 🔥 BIG PATTERN (VERY TESTED)

| Threat Actor    | Location          | Resources | Motivation      |
| --------------- | ----------------- | --------- | --------------- |
| Nation-state    | External          | Very High | Political / war |
| Unskilled       | External/Internal | Low       | Curiosity       |
| Hacktivist      | External          | Medium    | Ideology        |
| Insider         | Internal          | Medium    | Revenge / money |
| Organized crime | External          | High      | Money           |
| Shadow IT       | Internal          | Low       | Convenience     |

# 🧠 Memory Anchors (from concepts)

* Nation-state = government
* Script kiddie = no skill
* Hacktivist = political hacker
* Insider = already inside
* Organized crime = money
* Shadow IT = bypass rules
